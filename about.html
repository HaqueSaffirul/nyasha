Meet the studio

Designing immersive mehndi experiences for modern celebrations.

NYASHA Mehndi Artist is a Bhilai-based studio specializing in beautiful mehndi experiences for weddings, festivals, and special celebrations across India. We bring traditional artistry with a modern touch to make your special moments truly memorable.

Rooted in tradition, innovating for the future.

Founded by NYASHA Gautam in 2025, our studio blossomed from a heartfelt passion for mehndi artistry into a dedicated team of skilled designers and artists. We beautifully blend traditional Indian designs with contemporary styles, creating mehndi patterns that tell your unique story and celebrate our rich cultural heritage.

Our talented artists are trained in premium henna application techniques and modern design practices. With every celebration, we pour our heart into creating stunning mehndi designs that bring joy, beauty, and lasting memories. From intricate bridal patterns to festive designs, we ensure each stroke reflects the warmth and vibrancy of Indian traditions.

Studio ethos

Values that guide every stroke.

Intentional Design

Har ek design mein humara pyaar aur mehnat hai! Every motif is carefully crafted with love, blending traditional Indian patterns with modern aesthetics. Our extensive design collection includes beautiful bridal, festive, and contemporary styles that celebrate our rich cultural heritage.

Client Care

Aapki khushi hamari khushi hai! We provide personalized attention, regular updates, and complete support throughout your journey. From initial consultation to post-event care, we ensure you feel special and well-cared for every step of the way.

Quality & Tradition

We use premium quality organic henna sourced from trusted Indian suppliers, ensuring beautiful, long-lasting stains that honor traditional practices. Our commitment to quality and authenticity makes every mehndi application a celebration of Indian artistry.

How we work

Structured process for seamless celebrations.

01

Consultation & Design Selection

Hum aapki baat sunte hain! We sit with you to understand your vision, preferences, and the special occasion. Together, we explore traditional and modern design options, ensuring your mehndi reflects your unique style and celebrates your beautiful moments perfectly.

02

Design Finalization

We share design previews and discuss every detail with you - from pattern styles to application areas. Your happiness is our priority, so we make sure you're completely satisfied before we begin. Har detail par hum aapke saath hain!

03

Beautiful Application

On your special day, our skilled artists arrive with premium quality henna and all necessary supplies. With care and precision, we create stunning mehndi designs that will leave you and your loved ones mesmerized. Every stroke is applied with love and expertise!

04

Aftercare Support

Aapki care humari zimmedari hai! We provide you with helpful tips for getting the best stain color, product recommendations, and follow-up support. We want your mehndi to look absolutely beautiful and last as long as possible!
